About Us

Mind Horizon

Who We Are

Mind Horizon is an EdTech AI company founded in 2024 on a singular belief: that the next great leap for humanity is cognitive, not technological. We exist to make that leap possible for every person on earth.

Our flagship product, LearnZed, is an AI-powered education platform that delivers personalized, adaptive learning through a proactive predictive AI companion. We believe knowledge is a right — not a privilege — and we are building the infrastructure to prove it.

Our Story

2024

Mind Horizon continues to grow, deepening partnerships and expanding the reach of LearnZed to more learners.

Core Pillars

Flagship AI Model
Proactive Predictive AI Companion

The core intelligence behind LearnZed. Rather than waiting for a student to struggle, the AI companion predicts knowledge gaps before they form — proactively guiding each learner along the optimal path.

1:1 Tutor Ratio

Every learner gets their own AI tutor — infinitely patient, always available, and deeply personalized. No waiting, no one-size-fits-all curriculum.

Instant Feedback

Responses and corrections happen in real time. Learners build momentum instead of waiting days for graded work to return.

Infinite Horizon

There is no ceiling. LearnZed adapts as learners grow — from first principles to advanced mastery, the system expands with them.

Platform Layers

Predictive Learning Engine

The AI companion model that anticipates each learner's next challenge, proactively surfacing concepts and exercises before gaps become blockers.

Adaptive Content Delivery

Continuously models each learner's knowledge state and adjusts content difficulty, pacing, and style in real time.

Natural Language Tutoring

Engages learners in open-ended dialogue — answering questions, posing challenges, and explaining concepts at the right depth.

Curriculum Graph

A structured knowledge graph that maps concepts and their dependencies across every domain we support.

Progress Intelligence

Detailed analytics that surface patterns in learner behaviour, helping identify gaps before they become blockers.
